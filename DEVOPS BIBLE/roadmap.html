⚡ The Rules (Non-Negotiable)

01 One phase at a time. Never jump ahead. Finish GitHub Actions before touching Linux.
02 One Udemy course per topic. Don't buy 3 Docker courses "just in case." Pick one, finish it.
03 After each course section, come to Claude for quiz/doubts before moving to next section.
04 YouTube is supplement, not replacement. Watch Nana's video on a topic AFTER the Udemy section, not instead of it.
05 Practice > watching. If you watch 2 hours, you should practice for 2 hours. Minimum.
06 Push everything to GitHub. Every exercise, every project. This becomes your portfolio.
07 Get certified. AWS Cloud Practitioner and CKA are your two biggest resume boosters. Don't skip them.
Total estimated timeline: 7-9 months of consistent learning
Cost: ₹0 (Cognizant Udemy Business + YouTube + Claude)
Only paid items: AWS certification exam fees (~$100 each) when you're ready
